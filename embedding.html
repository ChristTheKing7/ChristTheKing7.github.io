Vortex is an offline appliacation that was develped to handle obesity and over weight in people.

The appliacation was developed by students of Makerere University including Ssembatya David, Kyagaba Jonah, Muwanguzi Cyrus, Mukisa Samuel and Othieno Bendict as a course work project.

The application is supported on both Android and iOS.
